ABOUT ME

I specialized in Science stream in my high school days by studying subjects like Biology, Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, English and my national language Dzongkha. After high school, I interned as teacher in Druk School where I was responsible for teaching Physics and Maths. After that I also interned in a private IT firm to gain my experience to work in the industry. I applied for a two-year course in North Metropolitan TAFE in Information Technology specializing in Programming to gain practical skills in software language.
